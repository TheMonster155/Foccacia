Focaccia whit Caramelized Onions, Caranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds whit this festive foccaica appetier topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoond instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

    • Method

    1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or a in a srand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl wehn mixing hand to incorporate the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually kned the the last of the flour..
